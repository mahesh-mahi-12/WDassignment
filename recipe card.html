TAMARIND RICE

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup tamarind paste (or 2-3 tablespoons tamarind concentrate)
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt
  • 2-3 curry leaves
  • Chopped coriander leaves, for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Prepare Tamarind Paste
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat
  3. add mustard seeds and let them splutter
  4. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle
  5. Add curry leaves and dry red chilies
  6. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well
  7. Pour in the tamarind paste mixture
  8. Stir well and bring to a boil
  9. Reduce heat and simmer for 5-7 minutes or until the paste thickens
  10. Add the cooked rice to the tamarind paste mixture
  11. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves
  12. Finally,the tamarind rice will get ready and we can serve it tothe people
